Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Do you accept walk-ins? +
Yes, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for weekends and special occasions, to ensure you get your preferred time and seating.
What is your dress code? +
Our dress code is smart casual. We want you to feel comfortable while maintaining an elegant atmosphere. We kindly ask guests to avoid athletic wear, beachwear, and flip-flops.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +
Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when making your reservation, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate your needs.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for dinner guests. There is also a public parking garage adjacent to the restaurant with validated parking for lunch guests.
Can I book a private event? +
Yes! We have private dining rooms available for events ranging from intimate dinners to corporate gatherings. Please contact our events team at events@chefskitchen.com or call us for more information and availability.
